Competition Overview

This two-day online coding competition is open to online and campus students. You'll go head-to-head with fellow innovators in a fast-paced HackerRank challenge built to sharpen technical skills and problem-solving.

Event Details

  • Dates: June 2026 (2-day event)
  • Format: Online via HackerRank
  • Who can join: Campus and online students

Prizes

  • 🏆 1st Place: Grand Prize + Recognition
  • 🥈 2nd Place: Runner-up Prize
  • 🥉 3rd Place: Merit Prize
  • 🎖️ Bonus recognition for creativity and clean code

Competition Guidelines

  • Use your registered HackerRank account for all submissions.
  • Submissions must be your own work.
  • Internet access for documentation is allowed unless a challenge states otherwise.
  • Code will be judged on correctness, efficiency, and style.
  • Respect timing windows; late submissions may not be accepted.

Before You Click Start ✓

  • ✅ Confirm your HackerRank login works.
  • ✅ Verify your internet connection and power setup.
  • ✅ Read all challenge instructions before coding.
  • ✅ Prepare your development environment and preferred language.
  • ✅ Keep a water bottle and notes nearby — pace yourself.
