Take On the Full Sail Code Challenge

Ready to put your programming skills to the test? Join us from Friday, June 26 at 6:00 PM ET through Saturday, June 27 at 11:59 PM ET for the Full Sail University Code Challenge.

This is a two-day online competition open to both online and campus students. Participants compete on HackerRank in a fast-paced, hands-on coding experience designed to sharpen technical skills, strengthen problem-solving abilities, and showcase creativity.

  • Unnecessary browser tabs and apps are closed
